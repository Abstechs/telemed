Learn About Parasitic Diseases

Malaria

Caused by Plasmodium parasites transmitted through mosquitoes. Symptoms include fever, chills, and fatigue. Early diagnosis and prompt treatment prevent complications.

Schistosomiasis

Also known as bilharzia, caused by parasitic worms in freshwater. Symptoms include rash, fever, chills, and later organ damage if untreated.

Helminth Infections

Intestinal worms like hookworms and roundworms cause anemia and malnutrition. Preventable with hygiene and early treatment.
